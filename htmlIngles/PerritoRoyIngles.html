üìç Where Was He Found?

Roy was found near the Industrial Park in Trujillo, wandering among mechanic workshops and spare parts storage areas. Despite the hostile environment, his energy stood out among the concrete and noise. Some workers began leaving him water and food, and then they contacted us so we could rescue him.

üíî What Was His Life Like Before?

It seems Roy spent several years on the street. Although he remained strong, his fur was dirty and he had some wounds on his paws from walking on hot and dangerous surfaces. He slept between cardboard boxes or in gaps between construction materials. Despite his condition, he always wagged his tail when someone showed him attention.

ü©∫ What Is His Current Condition?

Roy is approximately 3 years old and weighs 24 kilos. He is vaccinated, dewormed and in good health. He is a dog with a lot of energy and vitality, ideal for active families or people. He is fully ready to start a new chapter in a loving home.

üéæ What Does He Like to Do?

Roy is a real whirlwind of happiness. He loves running, jumping, playing with balls, sticks, or anything he can find. He enjoys long walks, especially if there are green areas where he can explore. After all the movement, he loves to lie on his back to receive belly rubs.

üë∂ How Does He Behave with People or Children?

He is a very affectionate and sociable dog. He greets newcomers enthusiastically and likes being surrounded by people. With children, he is patient, but due to his energy, it is better if he interacts with older children who can engage with him in an active and respectful manner.

üè° What Type of Home Would Be Ideal for Him?

Roy needs an active home, where there is space for him to move and people who enjoy playing with him and taking him on walks. A house with a yard or garden would be ideal. He is perfect for young families, athletes, or people looking for a loyal and adventurous companion.
