üìç Where Was He Found?

Pedro was found in the district of La Esperanza, in Trujillo, living in an abandoned lot near a deserted construction site. He was malnourished, with neglected fur, and very fearful of humans.

üíî What Was His Life Like Before?

For months, Pedro survived by eating leftover garbage and drinking water from puddles. Sometimes he was chased away with stones. He never had a safe place to sleep, and his only shelter was an old board under the rain. The neighbors contacted our shelter when they noticed he was limping on one paw.

üéæ What Does He Like to Do?

Now that he is safe, Pedro has shown how amazing he is. He loves to sunbathe in the garden, go for walks on the Huanchaco beach, and play with balls. He is a calm dog who enjoys sleeping close to those he loves.

üë∂ How Does He Behave with People or Children?

Pedro is very gentle and patient. He gets along well with small children and elderly people. He loves receiving pets and is especially protective of the most vulnerable members of the household.

üè° What Type of Home Would Be Ideal for Him?

Pedro needs a home with space to move, preferably with a garden or patio. Ideally, a loving family that gives him attention, daily walks, and quality time. He is perfect for those looking for a loyal and grateful companion.
