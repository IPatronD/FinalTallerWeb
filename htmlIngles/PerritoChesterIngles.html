🐾 The Story of Chester

📍 Where Was He Found?

Chester was found in a rural area of Laredo, Trujillo. He was lying by the side of the road, clearly exhausted and dehydrated. A local lady would leave him water and food scraps when she could, until she decided to contact us when she noticed Chester could barely get up.

💔 What Was His Life Like Before?

Based on his behavior and appearance, it seems Chester was a guard dog on a farm or ranch, and was abandoned when he got older. He spent his days under the sun, seeking shade or aimlessly walking around. His paws showed signs of chronic fatigue, and his expression was that of a tired soul but still hopeful.

🩺 What Is His Current Condition?

Chester is approximately 9 years old and weighs 28 kilos. Despite his age, he is stable and calm. He doesn't have any serious illnesses, but he requires basic care and a stress-free environment. He is dewormed and receives regular check-ups to ensure his well-being.

🎾 What Does He Like to Do?

Chester is a true sleepyhead. He loves resting in sunny spots, receiving gentle strokes — especially behind his ears — and eating peacefully at his regular times. He isn't very active, but he enjoys short walks where he can sniff around and walk at his own pace. He loves simply being accompanied.

👶 How Does He Behave with People or Children?

He is a very noble and grateful dog. He gets along well with adults and seniors. He may get stressed around young children if they are too lively or noisy, so we prefer a home without them. His temperament is submissive and quiet, and he only seeks affection and peace.

🏡 What Type of Home Would Be Ideal for Him?

Chester needs a calm home with little movement. He is ideal for older adults who can offer him a relaxed routine, love, and patience. He doesn't require large spaces, just a comfortable corner and someone to accompany him in this stage of his life. Chester deserves to spend his last years surrounded by love, care, and respect.
