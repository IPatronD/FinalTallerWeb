üìç Where Was She Found?

Alfa was found in a rural area of the district of Poroto, about 40 minutes from Trujillo. She was wandering around a farm, disoriented and showing clear signs of malnutrition. She was searching for food scraps among the corn plants and sought shelter under bushes when it was hot.

üíî What Was Her Life Like Before?

Locals mentioned that Alfa lived with a family who moved away and left her behind. Since then, she lived exposed to the weather and the danger of other animals. Although some neighbors tried to feed her leftovers, Alfa had no medical care or protection. She slept outdoors and had irritated skin from insects.

ü©∫ How Did She End Up in the Shelter?

She was rescued by a volunteer who visited the area. Noticing her condition, the volunteer decided to take her to the shelter. There, she received veterinary care, vaccinations, was dewormed, and sterilized. Over time, she gained weight, and her energy returned.

üéæ What Does She Like to Do?

Alfa is extremely playful. She loves running after balls, exploring gardens, playing with sticks, and chasing butterflies. She has a special affection for the open field, where she can run freely and dig little holes. She also enjoys splashing in small puddles after the rain.

üë∂ How Does She Behave with People or Children?

She is a loving, obedient, and very sociable dog. She gets excited when she sees new people and always seeks human contact. She is perfect for homes with children, as she is patient and loves to play. Additionally, she gets along well with other dogs and even calm cats.

üè° What Type of Home Would Be Ideal for Her?

Alfa needs a home with space where she can move freely, like a large patio or garden. She is ideal for active people who enjoy walking, biking, or outdoor activities. She adapts well inside the house, as long as she has exercise routines and constant affection.
