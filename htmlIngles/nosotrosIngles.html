About Us

At Colitas Felices, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. We are dedicated to connecting rescued animals with people willing to give them a second chance. We work closely with shelters and volunteers to ensure responsible adoption processes.

Our Mission

We are starting a journey filled with hope and commitment to animal welfare. Our mission is to rescue, protect, and give second chances to furry friends who have been abandoned or mistreated. We strive to provide them with a safe space where they receive love, care, and above all, the chance to find a family that truly values them. Through education and awareness, we aim to inspire more people to join this movement, promoting responsible adoption and respect for all living beings.

Our Vision

We dream of a future where no animal suffers from abandonment or abuse, and where love and empathy for them are core values in our society. Even though we're just beginning, we aim to build a caring community that works together to protect and care for the animals who need it most. Our goal is to grow, create a positive impact, and become a reference in animal rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption‚Äî ensuring that every furry friend gets the chance to live a dignified life full of love.

Our Goals

  • Rescue and Rehabilitation: Increase the number of rescued animals and ensure their full physical and emotional recovery.
  • Responsible Adoptions: Facilitate safe adoption processes, ensuring each furry friend finds a loving and suitable home.
  • Awareness and Education: Launch campaigns to promote respect and care for animals, raising community awareness about their well-being.
