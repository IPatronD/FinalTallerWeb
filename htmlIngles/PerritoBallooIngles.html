üìç Where Was He Found?

Balloo was rescued in the district of Florencia de Mora, in Trujillo. He was found hiding under an abandoned mototaxi near a local market. He had been alone for several days, searching for food among boxes and fruit scraps, always avoiding passersby and the noise of the area.

üíî What Was His Life Like Before?

Before his rescue, Balloo lived on the street, sleeping between cardboard boxes and feeling cold at night. Even though he is only 1 year old, he already knew what abandonment felt like. Despite his small size, he managed to stay safe, hiding from cars and larger dogs. His fur was dirty and tangled, but he never lost the spark in his bright eyes.

ü©∫ What Is His Current Condition?

Balloo weighs 7 kilos and is vaccinated, dewormed, and in good health. His size makes him ideal for small spaces, and his young energy makes him an active, curious, and lively companion.

üéæ What Does He Like to Do?

Balloo loves to explore. When he goes out for walks, he can't stop sniffing everything around him. He loves to chase butterflies, play with plush toys, and snuggle in soft blankets. He is playful and has a sparkling personality, always ready to share joyful moments with his family.

üë∂ How Does He Behave with People or Children?

He is very friendly and affectionate. He gets along great with children, adults, and also with other dogs. He is always wagging his tail and loves to receive cuddles or treats. He is perfect for homes where play, affection, and constant companionship are valued.

üè° What Type of Home Would Be Ideal for Him?

Balloo adapts very well to apartments or small houses. He is perfect for families with children, single people, or elderly adults. All he needs is love, playtime, and daily outings to explore the world at his own pace.
