Leo was seen roaming around for several days in a residential area of Trujillo. He slept on rooftops and only came down to look for food. He stayed near an empty house where he sought refuge at night.

üíî What Was His Life Like Before?

It is suspected that Leo was left behind by his previous owners. His protective behavior and the way he guarded the area where he slept showed a strong territorial sense. Though he was wary at first, he accepted help after several attempts.

At 5 years old and weighing 6 kilos, Leo is sterilized and healthy. He has a strong and loyal personality. He is an imposing cat with an attentive gaze and confident stride.

üéæ What Does He Like to Do?

Leo prefers to observe before acting. He enjoys being in elevated places and patrolling his surroundings. He is not excessively playful, but enjoys petting when he seeks it. He is a great silent guardian of the home.

He is loyal to the adults he knows but does not get along with other animals. He is recommended in homes where he will be the only cat. With calm children, he can coexist without issue if his space is respected.
